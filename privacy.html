KannadaKali Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

KannadaKali is a Kannada language learning app. The app works offline and stores learning progress on the user's device.

Data Stored On Device

KannadaKali may store lesson progress, practice preferences, theme settings, scores, streaks, and similar learning state locally on the device.

KannadaKali does not require account creation, sign in, or cloud sync.

Microphone

KannadaKali requests microphone access only for pronunciation practice. Microphone access starts only after the user begins a speaking challenge. KannadaKali does not save microphone recordings or upload recordings to a KannadaKali server.

Network Use

KannadaKali does not require internet access for core app behavior. Core learning content and pronunciation audio are bundled with the app. KannadaKali does not include Firebase, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or crash reporting SDKs in the current release build.

Sharing and Sale of Data

KannadaKali does not sell user data. KannadaKali does not share user learning progress, scores, preferences, or pronunciation recordings with third parties.

Local Data Deletion

Users can clear locally stored learning progress inside the app from Account Info > Privacy and data > Clear local data. Users can also remove local data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in Android settings.
